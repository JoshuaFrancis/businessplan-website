Why digital plans are different

A plan that uses brick-and-mortar metrics for a SaaS business does not pass a first read.

Revenue model literacy

Subscription revenue, usage-based pricing, freemium conversion, and affiliate models each project differently. We build the financial model for the actual pricing structure, not a simplified version of it.

Digital investor vocabulary

Angel investors and venture funds evaluating digital businesses ask about burn rate, runway, unit economics, and payback period — not gross margin and working capital. We write in the language of the audience.

Market sizing methodology

TAM/SAM/SOM analysis for digital markets requires a different approach. Addressable market in digital is not geography — it is user behavior and intent. We size digital markets correctly and cite sources investors recognize.
